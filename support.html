a few quick answers

is wired free?
yes, completely. no subscriptions, no paywalls, no locked features.
is my data private?
yes. everything stays on your device. nothing is collected, sent, or shared. you can also lock the app with Face ID.
how do i add a habit?
tap the plus on the today screen, give it a name and an emoji, choose how often you'd like to do it, and you're set. stuck? tap a quick idea and go.
i missed a few days - did i lose my progress?
no. wired has no streaks to break and no guilt. just pick things back up whenever you're ready.
how do i reset everything?
open settings and choose reset. it clears your habits and data from the device.
