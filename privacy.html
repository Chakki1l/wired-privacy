privacy policy

your data stays yours.

last updated · may 26, 2026
Wired does not collect, track, or share your data. Everything stays on your device. We have no servers, and we cannot see anything you do in the app.

what wired stores

Wired stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Habits you create (name, emoji, time of day, frequency, completion history)
  • Rewards you add (name, category)
  • Your app preferences (theme, language, sound settings, app lock settings)

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework in your app's private container. We do not have access to it.

analytics & tracking

Wired does not use analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising networks, or any third-party data collection. The app makes no network requests during normal use.

notifications

If you enable notifications, Wired schedules local reminders for your habits. These notifications are generated entirely on your device.

app lock & face id

If you enable App Lock, Wired uses iOS's LocalAuthentication framework to verify your identity. Face ID, Touch ID, and passcode data are managed by iOS and never accessible to Wired.

children

Wired is intended for users 13 years of age and older.

crisis & mental-health resources

Wired includes a Resources section with crisis support links. These are external services not affiliated with Wired.

your rights

You can delete all your data at any time through Settings → Data → Delete all data. You can also export your data as JSON through Settings → Data → Export my data.

contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact support.wired.app@gmail.com.

disclaimer

Wired is not a medical app and does not replace professional treatment for ADHD, anxiety, eating disorders, or any other condition. If you are struggling, please contact a healthcare professional.
